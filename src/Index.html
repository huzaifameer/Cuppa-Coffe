Espresso Ancestry

Coffee Cuppa, established in 2023 by SollyDZ Solutions, has quickly become a beloved institution in the scenic estates of Galle and Kandy. With a passion for crafting exceptional coffee experiences, the company embarked on a journey to blend the rich coffee traditions of Sri Lanka with innovative brewing techniques. Over the years, Coffee Cuppa has transformed into a cherished destination for locals and tourists alike, offering a warm and inviting atmosphere where the aroma of freshly roasted beans mingles with the laughter of patrons. It's a testament to our commitment to quality, community, and the enduring love for the perfect cup of coffee.

Based in the heart of Galle, Coffee Cuppa not only serves as a welcoming haven for coffee enthusiasts but also operates as a dynamic hub for both domestic and international coffee distribution. Our main office in Galle serves as the nerve center of our operations, meticulously overseeing the seamless flow of our exceptional coffee blends to customers far and wide. From cozy corners in our beloved homeland to distant shores across the globe, we take pride in delivering the essence of Sri Lankan coffee culture to all corners of the world. Our commitment extends beyond individual coffee cups; we offer wholesale orders, ensuring that the rich flavors of our coffee are available in caf√©s, restaurants, and homes everywhere. At Coffee Cuppa, we're not just brewing coffee; we're crafting connections that transcend borders.
